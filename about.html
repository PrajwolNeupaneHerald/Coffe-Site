History

Mero Coffee was founded in 1998 in the heart of Nepal, with a passion for bringing the rich and diverse coffee culture of Nepal to the world. The founders, two coffee enthusiasts who traveled the world in search of the perfect cup, discovered the unique flavors and aromas of Nepalese coffee and decided to share it with the world.

About

At Mero Coffee, we are dedicated to sourcing the highest quality coffee beans from the remote mountain regions of Nepal, where the rich soil and favorable climate produce some of the finest coffee in the world. Our beans are carefully roasted in small batches to preserve their unique flavor profile and aroma.
Our coffee shop offers a cozy and inviting atmosphere where customers can sit back, relax, and enjoy a cup of coffee or tea while soaking up the Nepalese culture. We also offer a selection of delicious snacks and desserts to complement your coffee experience.

In addition to our commitment to quality, we are also dedicated to sustainability and supporting local communities. We work closely with Nepalese farmers to ensure fair and ethical practices and to help build a better future for their families.
Come visit us at Mero Coffee and discover the rich and diverse coffee culture of Nepal, one cup at a time.
